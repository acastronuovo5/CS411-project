The gift that keeps on giving.

Struggling with a birthday gift, searching for a wedding gift, perhaps in need of anniversary gift? You have no clue where to start and that’s okay!

Welcome to the Tweet Store, your one-stop-shop that'll make your gift shopping a whole lot tweeter! Tweet Store is a web app that allows users to generate a list of stores based on Tweets pulled from a valid Twitter account. Just sign in with a Twitter account, enter in the Twitter account of the person you are searching a gift for, and let Tweet Store do the work. We’ll find you stores near you to start your search.

  1. Hit Login in the top right!
  2. Click Start The Search!
  3. Enter in the friend's Twitter and your city.
