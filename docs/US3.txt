US3 - Entering Location Information

- A user wants to give the app relevant information about their location so the app will be able to choose stores in the area with the gifts of a certain category.

- Once logging in and selecting a friend, the user will be prompted to enter their location (city) to locate a list of places to buy a gift from

- Once completed and filled in correctly, the application will move into the next step and display a waiting signal while the app looks for gift location suggestions.
